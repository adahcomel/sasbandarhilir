Dataran Pahlawan Melaka Megamall is a shopping mall located in Banda Hilir, Malacca City, Malacca, Malaysia. It is one of the properties owned by Hatten Group Sdn. Bhd., alongside nearby Hatten Square, Terminal Pahlawan, Hatten and Estadia Hotels and Hatten City Commercial Area. Mahkota Parade is a shopping mall in Malacca City, Malacca, Malaysia, which was originally established on 30 January 1994 and relaunched on 1 May 2010 after refurbishment. It was acquired by Hektar REIT (Real Estate Investment Trust) in 2006 and became one of its shopping centres, alongside Kulim Central and Central Square in Kedah, Wetex Parade and Segamat Central in Johor, and Subang Parade in Selangor. It houses stores of international brands, food-and-beverages outlets, entertainment centers, 10 Screen MM cineplexes, Ampang Super Bowl, karaoke etc.
