Accommodation

In Bandar Hilir community, there is a accomodation that are popular among visitors and Melaka people. In this page, we will show about the places within the picture and descriptions. We hope visitors and tourists can get some knowledge about Bandar Hilir community.

An 8-minute walk from revolving Taming Sari Tower, The elegant suites feature 2 flat-screen TVs, Wi-Fi, marble bathrooms, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. There are also minibars and sitting areas with sofas. Upgraded suites add separate living rooms and dining areas, and some add 2nd bedrooms. Room service is available. Breakfast is served in the buffet-style restaurant. There's a 22nd-floor lounge and a casual coffee shop. Other amenities include an infinity pool and poolside bar, plus a gym and a spa.

Hatten Hotel

This sophisticated hotel is 2 km from both Baba & Nyonya Heritage Museum and St. Paul's Church. Relaxed rooms offer free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and safes, plus marble bathrooms, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Suites add separate living areas. Some have kitchenettes and/or sofabeds. Room service is available. Parking is free. Dining options consist of a restaurant, a cocktail lounge and 2 cafes (1 is rooftop). Other amenities include a gym, a salt room and karaoke, plus an outdoor pool, a hot tub, and meeting and event space. Breakfast is available.

Imperial Heritage Hotel Melaka

In a modern high-rise across the street from the Malacca River. Featuring floor-to-ceiling windows and balconies with river and/or city views, the contemporary rooms come with free Wi-Fi and flat-screens, plus minibars, and tea and coffeemaking equipment. Suites add living rooms; some offer 2 bedrooms. Room service is offered. There's a bright restaurant with river views, along with a polished rooftop bar on the 30th floor. Additional amenities include 2 outdoor pools, a kids' pool and a gym. Covered parking is available.

Swiss Garden Hotel Melaka

Along National Road 5, this down-to-earth hotel is 11 minutes' walk from A Famosa, a 1500s fortress, and 12 minutes away on foot from the Melaka Sultanate Palace Museum. The warmly decorated rooms and family suites provide Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and safes, as well as minifridges, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Amenities consist of a cafe, and a rooftop pool featuring city views. Parking is also available.

Hallmark Crown Hotel

Off Federal Route 5, this hip boutique hotel in a restored redbrick building is 14 minutes' walk from the museum at the Malacca Sultanate Palace and 13 minutes on foot from the Straits of Malacca waterfront. Industrial-chic rooms with concrete accents feature free Wi-Fi, as well as flat-screen TVs, minibars, and tea and coffeemaking equipment. Upgraded rooms and family quarters add sitting areas. A trendy cafe has a garden terrace. There's also a gym.

Rosa Malacca

A 4-minute walk from historic Dutch Square and Stadthuys, a 17th-century Dutch Colonial structure with a museum, this hip hotel overlooking tropical gardens is 10 km from Melaka International Airport. Industrial-chic rooms provide flat-screens, free Wi-Fi, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Some include garden or pool views; 1 room features sleek bunk beds. Suites add in-room whirlpool baths, plus floor-to-ceiling windows facing a garden. Perks include parking and continental breakfast. A funky lounge features a game room and a stage. There's also an outdoor pool with a wooden sundeck and a covered terrace
