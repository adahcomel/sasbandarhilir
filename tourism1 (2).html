Tourism Places

In Bandar Hilir community, there is a tourism places that are popular among visitors and Melaka people. In this page, we will show about the places within the picture and decriptions. We hope visitors and tourists can get some knowledge about Bandar Hilir community.

River Cruise is one of the activity that can bring people to see wall arts and places around Bandar Hilir river with a cruise. For river cruise ticket it being divided by local group and foreigner group. For local group, 12 years old above it cost RM25.00 , 12 years below costs RM15.00. However for IPTA/IPTS Malaysia will be charged RM20.00

River Cruise

Jonker Walk is the Chinatown street of Melaka along Jonker Street. Jonker Walk is popular by their Night Market. It fulls of array of stalls, a friendly hawkers and colourful lively street party-like atmosphere. It open from Friday and Saturday from 6.00 p.m untill 12.00 p.m

Jonker Walk

Baba & Nyonya Heritage Museum is a World UNESCO Site. The house is a combination of three terrace lots that were acquired by the Chan family in 1861. Today, the museum is still managed by the family. The operating hours are from 10.00 a.m untill 5.00 p.m but extended hour on Friday, Saturday and Sunday from 10.00 a.m untill 6.00 p.m

Baba & Nyonya Heritage Museum

Melaka is Taming Sari Bhd (MTSB) is the subsidiary of Kumpulan Melaka Berhad (KMB) which wholly owned by Melaka State Government. Taming Sari is an experience of a breath-taking view from above. It also an experience from a height of 80 meters with a panaromic view of Historical Melaka City and afar

Taming Sari

The Pirate Adventure or known as Melaka Alive. It's a musical theatre show that's set on a ship inspired stage. It being held twice a day in the evening and at night. The theatre is about 600 years of history through special and visual effects,musing and live acting. After a succesful launch of Melaka Alive, a new 5D product called "The Pirate Adventure" that opened its doors to adventurous and fun seeking visitors.

Pirate Adventure

Melaka Art Gallery was established in 1958. There's around 150 paintings and 30 sculptures that were produced by famous Malaysian artists. It's open everyday from 9.00 a.m to 5.30 p.m and the fee is RM1.00

Melaka Art Gallery

The Tang Beng Swee Clock Towers was given to the individuals of Melaka in 1886. It's 135 year old red clock tower that were located at Dutch Square at Stadthuys. It also was built about 61 years after the Dutch surrendered Melaka to the British.

Clock Tower

Tan Kim Seng Bridge is the bridge across the Melaka River. It were located near the The Tang Beng Swee Clock Tower.

Tan Kim Seng Bridge

Rumah Terbalik or Upside Down house is a furniture that were built upside down. From a home room, bathroom and everything were built in the ceiling.

Rumah Terbalik
