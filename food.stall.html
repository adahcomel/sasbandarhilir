Food

“Malacca is a food heaven!” is what you’ll most likely hear when you enquire about the small and historical city. Tantalize your taste buds with all these mouthwatering selections of food; spicy, sweet, tangy and sour local specialties all over the streets of Malacca. So here have been attached some popular restaurants and stalls in Melaka that offer interesting food dishes for all the visitors.

Cendol Jam Besar stall is located in Bandar Hilir, 75200 Melaka, facing the The Stadthuys building. Among the menus that visitors choose are cendol kacang and cendol durian. The stall operates every Thursday to Friday only, starting at 10 a.m. to 7 p.m.

Cendol Jam Besar

Cafe Dutch Harbor is located at Jalan Laksamana, Bandar Hilir, 75100 Melaka. The cafe offers desserts and western food such as spaghetti, cake and chicken chop. The cafe operates every day except Wednesdays from 4 a.m to 11.30p.m for Monday to Friday and on weekends from 11 a.m to 11.30p.m.

Dutch Harbour Cafe

Cendol Kampung Hulu is located at Jalan Kampung Hulu, Kampung Hulu, 75200 Melaka. The restaurant offers various types and choices of cendol for customers and the most popular is the original shaved ice cendol. The restaurant operates daily from 11 a.m. to 11 p.m..
