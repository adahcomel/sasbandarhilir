In Bandar Hilir community, there is a entertainment places that visitors and Melaka people can enjoy and fulls their labor time. In this page, we will show about the places within the picture and decriptions. We hope visitors and tourists can get some knowledge about Bandar Hilir community.

The trishaws in Melaka are elaborately decorated with artificial flowers, cartoons and noisy music that were playing under the seat. The trishaw can be found around Dutch-era Stadthuys and Christ Church. The price are around RM40 per hour.
